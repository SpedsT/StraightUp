Tips

Physical activity
It is recommanded that once every hour there should be a minimum of physical activity of any kind.
Chair
The back of the chair should be tilted at about 100-110 degrees.
The lumbar and thoracic area of the back should be rested against the chair so that the weight should be evenly distributed.
Legs
The legs should have a flat contact with the ground so that the angle between the legs and the chair should be around 100-110 degrees.
Between the back of the knees and the chair should be a safe space of a few centimeters so that the blood circulation is not restricted
Screen
The screen position is determined with a test. You should sit in a correct and relaxed position on the chair with your eyes closed so you don't get biased by the position of the screen. After you open your eyes the screen should be right in the center of your vision. If it is not it should be repositioned.
The distance between the eye and the screen should be an arm's lenght away but it depends on your eye sight.
The reflections off the screen should be avoided by tilting the screen down and removing any shining objects around.
Arm rests
The arm rests should be positioned in such a way that the shoulder muscles should be relaxed.
Keyboard and mouse
The keyboard and mouse should be placed in such a way that the shoulder muscles should be relaxed, the elbows resting on the table and the palm joints to be straight and relaxed.
